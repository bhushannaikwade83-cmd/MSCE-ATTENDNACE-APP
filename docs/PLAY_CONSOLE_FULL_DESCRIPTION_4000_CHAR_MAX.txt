MSCE GCC-TBC Attendance supports authorised institute staff in recording and reviewing learner attendance consistently with procedures approved for your centre under the Maharashtra State Council of Examination (MSCE) GCC-TBC attendance programme.

WHO THIS APP IS FOR
The application is intended for registered GCC-TBC institutes and authorised personnel—institute administrators and designated attendance-only staff—after provisioning by administration. Access is institute-scoped: users should only handle data belonging to their own centre.

DAY-TO-DAY WORKFLOW (AT A HIGH LEVEL)
Maintain an up-to-date learner roster aligned with institute records; locate students quickly via roster search during peak periods; mark attendance using the workflows your administration enables.

KEY FEATURES
Student roster with search; attendance marking with location verification when your institute has configured an approved GPS attendance zone; role-based access for administrators and attendance-only staff; daily attendance visibility and reporting for supervisory review subject to institute policy; institutional sign-in and session handling suited to supervised environments.

GEOLOCATION AND DEVICE USE
Location may be required when your institute’s policy mandates a GPS attendance zone. If checks are enforced, marking may not be permitted until institute-defined conditions are met. Coordinators should verify device permissions, time/date accuracy, and that the institute zone has been properly locked according to administrator instructions.

CAMERA USE (WHERE ENABLED)
Camera access may apply only where your institute activates verification steps (for example confirming presence during marking). Follow institute instructions for acceptable use.

DATA AND PRIVACY
Handling of operational data follows your published Privacy Policy linked in-store. Administrators are responsible for safeguarding credentials (including PINs for attendance staff), updating access when roles change, and meeting institute information-handling obligations.
