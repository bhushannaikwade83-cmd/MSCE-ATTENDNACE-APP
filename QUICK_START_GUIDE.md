# MSCE Attendance System - Quick Start Guide

## 1️⃣ Sign Up / Login

### First Time Setup
1. Open the app
2. Click **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter email and password
4. Verify institute code
5. Login with credentials

### Login
1. Open the app
2. Enter email and password
3. Click **"Login"**
4. Select institute

---

## 2️⃣ Student Face Registration

### Register Student
1. Go to **"Student Management"**
2. Click **"Add Student"**
3. Enter student name and roll number
4. Take **CLOSE-UP PHOTO** of student's face:
   - Face must fill 8-10% of frame
   - Good lighting, clear face
   - Eyes open, looking at camera
   - Only one person
5. System stores face data
6. Student ready for attendance

---

## 3️⃣ Marking Attendance

### Entry Photo
1. Go to **"Mark Attendance"**
2. Select student from list
3. Ensure GPS is enabled (location within institute radius)
4. Take **ENTRY PHOTO** (close-up):
   - Face fills frame (8-10% minimum)
   - Clear lighting
   - Looking at camera
5. System verifies face matches student
6. ✅ Entry recorded

### Exit Photo
1. When student leaves, click **"Mark Exit"**
2. Take **EXIT PHOTO** (same requirements as entry):
   - Close-up photo
   - Clear face
   - Looking at camera
3. If within 2.5 hours: ✅ Exit recorded
4. If after 2.5 hours: ⏰ Auto-closed (1 hour credit given)

---

## 4️⃣ Reports

### Generate Report
1. Go to **"Reports"**
2. Select date range
3. Choose **"All Students"** or **"Defaulters"**
4. Click **"Load"**

### Download PDF
1. Click on student name
2. Click **"View PDF Report"** or **"Download"**
3. PDF shows daily attendance with times and hours

### Report Shows
- Date, Subject, Entry Time, Exit Time
- Seated hours or "No exit photo (1.0 h credited)"
- Status: Present / Absent / Holiday

---

## 5️⃣ Holiday Close/Open

### Mark Holiday (Close)
1. Go to **"Institute Settings"** or **"Holiday Management"**
2. Click **"Add Holiday"**
3. Select date
4. Enter reason (optional)
5. Click **"Save"**
6. Date marked as holiday - no attendance recorded

### Remove Holiday (Open)
1. Go to **"Holiday Management"**
2. Find holiday date
3. Click **"Delete"** or **"Remove"**
4. Holiday removed - attendance can now be marked

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Face Photo Requirements
- **CLOSE-UP ONLY** - Face must fill significant portion of frame
- Will REJECT if face is too small/distant
- Good lighting, eyes visible, one person only

### Auto-Close Policy
- Student doesn't exit within 2.5 hours = Auto-closed
- Automatic 1 hour credit given
- Report shows: "No exit photo (1.0 h credited)"

### GPS Requirement
- Attendance marked within institute radius only
- Turn off Mock Location if testing
- Allow GPS to stabilize before marking
